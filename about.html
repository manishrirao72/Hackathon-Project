Steps involved in booking a service

These steps will make your journey easy in our website for booking a service

Step 1

Click on services button.

Step 2

Select your location and add two images of your vehicle problem and click on next button.

Step 3

The nearby shops are appeared now select any service center and press view full details.

Step 4

You can see full details of service center.Now you can book your service by clicking place order

Step 5

Enter your information and place your order.
